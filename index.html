Click here to download my CV.

Research Interests

My research spans over human-robot interaction, human-centred AI, and affective computing.
My work focuses on adopting interdisciplinary knowledge to develop robots that incorporate social-emotional intelligence in their perception, decision, and actions, thus enhancing their communication and collaboration with people in various applications.

Miscellaneous

I am a Manchurian Chinese (Bordered Yellow Banner) from Beijing, China.
My name 田㶟旻 is pronounced as "tián lěi mín" in pinyin:
  田 (tián) is my family name. It means the field or the earth
  㶟 (lěi) is the ancient name of the Yongding River that flows through Beijing, where my family lives next to. It also means water.
  旻 (mín) means autumn or the sky. I was born at the start of autumn.
My name can be loosely translated as "Someone from the Tian family living next to the Lei river who was born in autumn." And by including the earth (Tian), the water (Lei), and the sky (Min) in my name, I am free to explore the whole wide world 🌏
